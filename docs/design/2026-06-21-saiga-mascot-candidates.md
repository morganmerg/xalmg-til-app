## Cultural Correction

The earlier generated scarf/ornament language drifted toward Kazakh-style blue-gold geometric loops. Do not reuse that direction.

Use Kalmyk references instead:

- Kalmykia emblem: blue/yellow/red palette, lotus petals, zala/radiating red form, circular framing.
- Kalmyk costume: red/black garment fields, thin gold embroidery, gold trim, small tassel accents.
- Mascot application: keep these as small collar/scarf/detail accents, not a full human costume.

Prompt-level avoid list:

- no Kazakh loop ornament;
- no generic Turkic geometric pattern;
- no yurt/carpet motifs;
- no hats or full folk costume on the mascot;
- no exact copy of the Kalmykia emblem.
