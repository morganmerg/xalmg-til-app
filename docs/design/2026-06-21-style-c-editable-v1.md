## Direction

Keep the playful mascot-first energy, but avoid direct Duolingo cloning. The v1 direction uses:

- deep blue app chrome;
- yellow daily quest / reward surfaces;
- coral-red feedback state;
- ivory background;
- simple lotus/zala accents;
- juvenile saiga mascot as a raster asset inside editable UI cards.
